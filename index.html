What We Do
SERVICES

Specialised automotive electronics work. Each job is measured, diagnosed, and executed precisely — not approximated. We work with the data, not around it.

01
ECU / TCU Cloning

Full duplication of engine and transmission control units. Damaged, waterlogged, or dead unit — data is transferred to a donor with precision. No dealer re-coding required.

ECU TCU DSG KTAG
02
IMMO & Key Systems

Immobilizer bypass, IMMO-off, key synchronization. CAS, EWS, BSI, BCM — we work at chip level, not through OBD shortcuts.

CAS EWS BSI IMMO-OFF
03
EEPROM / Flash

Read, write, backup and restore EEPROM and Flash memory. Mileage data, VIN, configuration — extracted and rewritten accurately.

EEPROM FLASH READ/WRITE
